💡 TERMINOLOGY NOTE
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
"Transactions" = specimen movements and activities
  • Loans (temporary transfers to institutions)
  • Photography (documentation sessions)
  • Exhibitions (public display periods)
  • Sales/Trades (permanent transfers)

This is standard museum collection management terminology
(similar to EMu, Specify, Arctos, and other professional systems).

NOT financial transactions.
