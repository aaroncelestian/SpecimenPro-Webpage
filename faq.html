Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about organizing your collection

Getting Started

Q: What can I catalog with SpecimenPro?

Anything you collect from nature! Rocks, minerals, fossils, insects, shells, pressed plants, feathers—if you collect it, you can catalog it. Our flexible system adapts to whatever information matters to your hobby.

Popular collections include:

  • Mineral and rock collections
  • Insect collections (butterflies, beetles, etc.)
  • Fossil collections
  • Shell collections
  • Pressed plant herbaria
  • Feather collections
  • Any natural history specimens
Q: I'm not a scientist. Is this too complicated for me?

Not at all! SpecimenPro is designed for collectors of all experience levels. If you can take a photo with your iPhone, you can catalog specimens.

Many users are hobbyists who just want to organize and remember their finds. The app has professional features if you need them, but you can start simple and add more detail as you go.

Q: How do I get started?

Getting started is easy:

  1. Download SpecimenPro from the App Store
  2. Launch the app—demo data loads automatically
  3. Explore the demo collection to see how it works
  4. Create your own collection when you're ready
  5. Start adding your finds with photos and notes

No setup required—just start cataloging!

Q: Do I need to be a museum or institution?

Nope! SpecimenPro is built for individual collectors. Whether you have 20 specimens or 2,000, it scales to your personal collection.

While some museums and researchers use it, the app is designed for anyone who loves collecting. You don't need institutional credentials or special training.

Importing Your Collection

Q: Can I import my existing collection list?

Yes! Upload any CSV or Excel file. We read your column headers and automatically create matching fields. No template required, no reformatting needed.

Import Features:
  • Flexible CSV/Excel import
  • Reads any column headers
  • Automatically maps your fields
  • Import hundreds of specimens at once

Note: Importing large collections requires the full version ($9.99) since the free version is limited to 30 specimens total.

Q: What if I have hundreds of photos to add?

Use our batch photo import! Just name your photos with catalog numbers (like "MIN-001.jpg") and upload the whole folder. SpecimenPro automatically links each photo to the right specimen.

This saves hours of manual work and lets you import your entire photo library at once.

Q: Can I export my data if I want to switch systems later?

Absolutely. Export to CSV anytime with one click. Your data is never locked in.

Export options include:

  • CSV: Open in Excel or Google Sheets
  • SQLite: Full database for analysis
  • ZIP Archive: Complete collection with all photos

Your data stays yours, always.

Features & How-To

Q: Does it work offline?

Yes, completely! SpecimenPro is designed to work anywhere, even with no cell service. Perfect for field collecting in remote locations.

Everything works offline:

  • Add new specimens
  • Take photos
  • Record voice notes
  • Use AR measurements
  • Search your collection

When you're back online, everything syncs automatically if you have iCloud enabled.

Q: How do QR codes work?

QR codes give you instant access to specimens and locations:

  • Specimen QR Codes: Generate a code for each specimen. Scan it to open that specimen instantly.
  • Location QR Codes: Create codes for drawers or cabinets. Scan to see everything stored there.
  • Print & Attach: Print codes and stick them on storage containers for quick access.

It's like having a barcode scanner for your collection!

Q: Can I track where I found specimens?

Yes! SpecimenPro captures GPS coordinates automatically when you add specimens in the field. You can:

  • See find locations on a map
  • Add location names and notes
  • View all specimens from a specific area
  • Track your collecting trips over time

Never forget where you found that perfect specimen!

Q: How does AR measurement work?

Point your iPhone camera at a specimen and get instant measurements using augmented reality. No ruler needed!

  1. Open any specimen and tap "AR Measurement"
  2. Point your camera at a flat surface
  3. Place measurement points on your specimen
  4. Get length, width, height, and volume automatically

Perfect for measuring delicate specimens without touching them.

Q: Can I customize what information I track?

Absolutely! Add custom fields to track whatever matters to you:

  • GPS coordinates for rocks
  • Host plants for insects
  • Dive depth for shells
  • Formation names for fossils
  • Collection dates and locations
  • Anything else you want to remember

Your collection, your way.

Sharing & Collaboration

Q: Can I share my collection with other collectors?

Yes! Export your database and share it with fellow collectors. They can import it into their own SpecimenPro app.

Great for:

  • Sharing with collector friends
  • Trading specimens (track what went where)
  • Showing your collection to clubs or groups
  • Teaching others about your finds
Q: Can I track loans and trades?

Yes! Keep track of specimens you've loaned to friends or traded with other collectors:

  • Record who has what
  • Set return dates with reminders
  • Save contact information
  • View complete history

Never lose track of your loaned specimens again!

Privacy & Data Security

Q: What data does SpecimenPro collect about me?
None. Zero. Nothing.
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No third-party SDKs
  • No ads
  • No data collection of any kind

Your collection data stays on your device or in your private iCloud. I can't see your data and don't want to.

Q: How does iCloud sync work?

iCloud sync is optional and private:

  • Syncs between your own devices only
  • Uses your personal iCloud account
  • I have no access to your iCloud data
  • You can disable it anytime
  • Automatic conflict resolution

Your data never passes through any third-party servers.

Q: What happens to my data if I stop using the app?

Your data stays yours forever:

  • Export everything to CSV before you leave
  • Open your exports in Excel, Google Sheets, or any database
  • Keep all your photos and files
  • No data is lost or locked away

You're never trapped in SpecimenPro. Your collection data is portable.
