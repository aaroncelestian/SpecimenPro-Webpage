Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SpecimenPro

Getting Started

Q: How do I get started with SpecimenPro?

Getting started is easy:

  1. Download SpecimenPro from the App Store
  2. Launch the app—demo data loads automatically
  3. Tap "Demo Mineral Collection" to explore 5 pre-loaded specimens
  4. Try scanning the QR codes to see how quick access works
  5. Create your own collection when you're ready

The demo collection includes complete documentation examples to help you understand all features.

Q: Does SpecimenPro work offline?

Yes, completely! SpecimenPro is designed with an offline-first approach, making it perfect for field research in remote locations.

Key Points:
  • No internet connection required for any features
  • All data stored locally on your device
  • iCloud sync is optional and can be disabled
  • Camera, AR measurements, and voice notes all work offline

Features & Functionality

Q: What are "Transactions" in SpecimenPro?

Transactions track specimen movements and activities—this is standard museum collection management terminology used in professional systems like EMu, Specify, and Arctos.

Transaction Types:
  • Loans: Temporary transfers to institutions or individuals
  • Photography: Documentation sessions
  • Exhibitions: Public display periods
  • Sales/Trades: Permanent transfers

Note: These are NOT financial transactions—they track specimen history and location.

Q: How do QR codes work?

QR codes provide instant access to specimens and locations:

  • Specimen QR Codes: Generate unique codes for each specimen. Scanning opens that specimen directly.
  • Location QR Codes: Create codes for physical locations (cabinets, drawers). Scanning filters your collection to show only specimens in that location.
  • Quick Access: Print codes and attach them to storage containers or specimen labels for instant retrieval.
Q: Can I export my data?

Yes! Your data is never locked in. SpecimenPro offers multiple export formats:

  • CSV: Open in Excel, Google Sheets, or import into other systems
  • SQLite: Full database export for analysis in Python, R, or other tools
  • ZIP Archive: Complete collection with all photos, files, and a README

All exports include complete metadata and are designed for professional use.

Q: How does AR measurement work?

SpecimenPro uses Apple's ARKit to capture accurate 3D dimensions:

  1. Open any specimen and tap "AR Measurement"
  2. Point your camera at a flat surface to establish a reference plane
  3. Place measurement points on your specimen
  4. The app calculates length, width, height, and volume

This provides non-contact measurements perfect for delicate specimens.

Q: Can I add custom metadata fields?

Yes! Each collection can have custom metadata fields tailored to your needs. This allows you to track:

  • Locality information
  • Acquisition details
  • Scientific classifications
  • Condition notes
  • Any other data relevant to your collection

Privacy & Data Security

Q: What data does SpecimenPro collect?
None. Zero. Nothing.
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No third-party SDKs
  • No external network requests
  • No data collection of any kind

Your data stays on your device or in your private iCloud account. The developer cannot access your data.

Q: How does iCloud sync work?

iCloud sync is optional and private:

  • Data syncs directly between your own devices via your private iCloud account
  • The developer has no access to your iCloud data
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time
  • Automatic conflict resolution when you edit on multiple devices

Your data never passes through any third-party servers.

Note: Only one user can access a database at a time. For team collaboration, see the database sharing question below.
Q: Where is my data stored?

All data is stored in a SQLite database on your device:

  • Local Storage: Primary database on your iPhone/iPad
  • iCloud (Optional): Encrypted backup in your private iCloud account
  • Exports: You control where exported files go

The database uses foreign key constraints and transaction-based operations to ensure data integrity.

Technical Questions

Q: What devices are supported?

SpecimenPro runs on:

  • iPhone (iOS 16.0 or later)
  • iPad (iPadOS 16.0 or later)

AR measurement features require devices with ARKit support (iPhone 6s or later, iPad 5th generation or later).

Q: How much storage does the app use?

The app itself is small, but storage depends on your collection:

  • App Size: ~50 MB
  • Photos: Compressed to save space while maintaining quality
  • Database: Very efficient—metadata uses minimal space
  • Attachments: Depends on what you attach (PDFs, voice notes, etc.)

A collection with hundreds of specimens typically uses less than 1 GB.

Q: Can I use SpecimenPro on multiple devices?

Yes! Your purchase works on all your devices signed in with the same Apple ID.

  • Enable iCloud sync to keep your collections synchronized across your devices
  • Or use each device independently with local storage
  • Export/import to move collections between devices manually

Note: iCloud sync is for your personal devices only. Only one user can access a database at a time.

Q: Can I share my collection database with other users or team members?

Yes, through export and import! While only one user can access a database at a time, you can share collections with others:

  1. Export your collection as a SQLite database
  2. Share the file via Dropbox, email, or any file-sharing method
  3. Other users can import the database into their SpecimenPro app
  4. The app includes automatic conflict resolution when importing
Perfect for:
  • Research teams sharing specimen data
  • Museum departments collaborating on collections
  • Field teams consolidating observations
  • Backing up and distributing collection snapshots

This workflow ensures data integrity while allowing flexible collaboration through standard file-sharing tools.

Q: What file formats can I attach to specimens?

You can attach most common file types:

  • Documents: PDF, DOC, DOCX, TXT
  • Spreadsheets: XLS, XLSX, CSV
  • Images: JPG, PNG, HEIC
  • Audio: Voice notes recorded in-app

Who Uses SpecimenPro?

Absolutely! The flexible design works for any collection type. Users have successfully used SpecimenPro for:

  • Art collections
  • Antiques and collectibles
  • Home inventory for insurance
  • Equipment tracking
  • Library cataloging

The custom metadata fields adapt to any collection type.
