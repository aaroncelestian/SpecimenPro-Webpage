About SpecimenPro

SpecimenPro helps labs and field researchers track, manage, and organize specimens with ease using your iPhone or iPad.
Streamline your workflow, reduce errors, and ensure accurate records.

Features

  • Intuitive specimen data entry and editing
  • Barcode scanning and quick search
  • Secure cloud sync and backup
  • Export data to CSV
  • Customizable fields for different specimen types
  • Voice note attachments
  • Draw directly on specimen images
